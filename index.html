Starbucks Drinks Data

Need a pick-me-up to keep you moving through your day? Take a look at the circle packing graph below to see Starbucks' signature drinks organized by caffeine content. Check out the non-caffeinated section below. For each graph, click on a bubble to load a stacked bar chart below of the nutrition information for that drink.
